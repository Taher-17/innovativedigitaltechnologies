NCEA Study

NCEA Study provides you with past papers for the following subjects: Maths, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. NCEA Study will also let you access these papers whenever and wherever because the past papers are available offline and on iPhone and iPad.

NCEA Study is not affiliated with NCEA or NZQA in any way.

Privacy Policy

NCEA Study does not collect or store any personal information. NCEA Study will collect anonymous information to determine whether or not you have bought the Remove Ads option. This information includes the receipt of your purchase and a random identifier when you install the app. This information cannot and is not linked to you.
